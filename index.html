Open Call

‘We begin with a squiggle’
Exchange and Workshop
@ MMOMA education center
6-10 May, 2019

From 6th until 10th of May 2019 a collaborative workshop between graphic design students from Moscow (1) and the Rietveld Academie in Amsterdam (2) will take place at the Moscow Museum of Modern Art Education Center (3).

The workshops will be guided by a group of international designers from Amsterdam and Moscow (4). We will begin with a squiggle (5).

Graphic design students and recent graduates in Moscow are invited to apply with a motivation letter (6) and five to ten images (7) to participate in the workshop. Please send your letter and works to xx@xx.ru by April 15th.

‘We begin with a squiggle’ is organised by David Bennewith (NZ) and Claudia Doms (GER) (8).
